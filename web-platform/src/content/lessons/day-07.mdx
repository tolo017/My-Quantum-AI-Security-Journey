# Day 7: Hashing, Digital Signatures, & PKI

Yesterday we learned how to hide data. Today, we learn how to prove data hasn't been changed (**Integrity**) and prove who it came from (**Authenticity**).

## Key Concepts
- **Hashing (The "Digital Fingerprint"):** A one-way function. You can turn a file into a hash, but you can't turn a hash back into the file.
- **Digital Signatures (The "Seal of Trust"):** Combines Hashing and Asymmetric encryption to verify identity and message content.
- **PKI (Public Key Infrastructure):** The system of Certificate Authorities (CAs) that allows everyone on the internet to securely trust public keys.
