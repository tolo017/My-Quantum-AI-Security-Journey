# The Importance of Reconnaissance

## What is Footprinting?
> `The first phase of ethical hacking and penetration testing. Also known as reconnaissance.`

## The 90/10 Rule
If a hacker spends 2 months on Recon and only 2 minutes on the actual Exploitation, were they a good hacker or a lucky hacker? Why?
> `A highly skilled hacker. The hacker took time to study the target, by the time they launched the exploit, they know exactly the vulnerabilities of the sytem, payloads to use, and how the sytem will react.`
